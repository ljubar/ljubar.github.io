About Me

This is what I do.

I am web developer at Sourcefabric. In implementations department, I work with clients on implementing their publishing platforms using Superdesk and more specifically Superdesk Publisher.

Being an architect by profession, I worked for years at City planning office in Valjevo, Serbia.

In free time I grow vegetables (some herbs and fruits as well) at Good-looking Gardeners. Also I am half-marathon runner.

My hobby is photography (Canon 70D * EF-S 50/1.8 * EF-S 28-135/3.5/5.6); I put my albums on Flickr since 2004. My typical use-case is 'Day in the mountains' or 'Family vacation', occasional business trips as well.

I am father of Sofija and Pavle.
