Ekspert. Tema: Sex & City

petek, 7. oktober 2005. u 12:09 PM

Piše: La Lara

Rubrike: Tabloid Buzz

Resavala sam kviz u Glamour Magazinu, tj. na njihovom sajtu, o Sex&City. Moj skor bio je 17 od 20. Rekli su mi: “You know what that means? That means you know more about this subject than 77 per cent of people who completed this quiz.” Nije lose.

Pitanja na koja nisam znala odgovore su sledeca:

What was the name of Aiden’s beloved dog?
Who plays Enid, the editor of US Vogue?
What is the name of Miranda’s short-lived lover from Weight Watchers?
(Nije ni cudo: Ne razumem se u glumce, a ni u zivotinje).
